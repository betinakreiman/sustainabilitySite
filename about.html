About Us

  We are a group of driven, passionate students at Newton North High School, working to create more sustainable practices at our school, and in our world, to better our environment. In addition to our goals of improving the impact that schools have on the environment, another priority of ours is ensuring that information about climate change is available and easily accessible for all.
  At our school, we have successfully implemented an anaerobic digestion food waste system. We have also taken on several other sustainable projects, such as reducing the use of non-compostable and non-reusable materials expended in the distribution of school lunches
